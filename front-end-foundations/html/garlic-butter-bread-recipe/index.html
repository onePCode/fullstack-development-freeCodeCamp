Garlic Butter Pasta

A quick and delicious pasta dish with fragrant garlic, rich butter, and a hint of fresh parsley. Perfect for busy weeknights or a simple comfort meal.

Ingredients

  • 200 g spaghetti (or your preferred pasta)
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve ½ cup of the pasta water, then drain.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil and butter over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant (about 1–2 minutes).
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the pan, along with a splash of pasta water. Toss well to coat.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley and Parmesan cheese if desired.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
